Zinda by Siddharth Mahadevan

About the Song

"Zinda" is a timeless song written and performed by Siddharth Mahadevan. Released in 2013, it has become one of the most iconic and influential songs of all time. The lyrics encourage listeners to envision a world of peace, unity, and harmony, free from the barriers that divide humanity.

About the Artist

Siddharth Mahadevan is an Indian playback singer from Mumbai, India. He is the son of popular music composer and singer Shankar Mahadevan. Siddharth has gained fame in the Bollywood music industry for his melodious voice and unique style of singing.
